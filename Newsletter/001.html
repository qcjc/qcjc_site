From the perspective of algorithmic designers, it is a marvelous revolution to discover how algorithms run on real machines. There are whole worlds behind the implementation of a quantum computer. Each hardware has its advantages and disadvantages. The interesting question that quantum computing brought to the surface is how we link the algorithmic procedure with the physical implementation of our tool. Studying quantum computing means diving into the possibilities of a physical system to do something that has never been made before. In constructing algorithms, we usually use gates as they are achievable, but there is a whole world of difficulties behind them.
