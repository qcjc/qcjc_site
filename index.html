Quantum Computing Journal Club

Based in Turin, Italy, we study quantum computing and information.

About Us

The Quantum Computing Journal Club is a group of researchers and enthusiasts who are interested in studying quantum computing and information. We meet regularly to discuss recent papers and developments in the field.

Our goal is to create a welcoming and supportive community where people can learn about and discuss quantum computing in a friendly and informal setting.
